Burger

Description

This is a burger recipe with a lot of meat. Added some sauces and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup mustard
  • 1/2 cup pickles
  • 1/2 cup relish
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 1/2 cup tomato
  • 1/2 cup lettuce

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef
  2. Add the mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, pickles, relish, onion, tomato, and lettuce
  3. Serve with a bun
